Origin

I began dog walking in high school in 2017. Over the years it went from a side gig to make money during school, to a true passion of mine. I strive to provide each pet with the best possible care and I make sure to keep the puppy-parents in the loop throughout. I let them know when I am with their pup, and when I leave I send a detailed update along with a cute image of their furry friend. I want clients to feel relieved to know their pets are being treated with the same love that they would give them while away on vacation or stuck at work.
